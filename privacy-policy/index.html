Privacy Policy

Collected data

In order to estimate the amount of visitors on the site and the amount of downloads, Piwik is used to track your activity on the site.
This tracking may happen with or without javascript and may involve cookies. But this data will not be shared with any third-party.

For experimental purposes, other trackers may be added to the site. These third-party trackers may share your data.
This data includes visited pages, your user-agent, browser information, ...

Opt-out

I do not provide a simple opt-out because I recommend disabling trackers everywhere instead of just on my site.

I highly recommend you to install Firefox and have a look at the following add-ons:

  • Blend In
  • DoNotTrackMe
  • Ghostery
  • NoScript
  • ...
I also recommend making your browser send the Do Not Track header.

Policy changes

This privacy policy can change at any time without a warning.
However, because the site is open-source and hosted on Github Pages, you can follow all changes on github.
